02 — THE WORK WE MAPPED

Your current reality — and where the gaps are.

You're not failing. You're succeeding despite having no infrastructure. But every missed call while you're at practice, every lead that goes cold because you couldn't follow up, every "I'll think about it" that never got a second touch — that's money walking out the door. Here's what we see:

1

Lead Generation bottleneck

You're running on word of mouth only. No ads, no SEO, no presence when someone Googles "pool builder Magnolia TX." The market is exploding and you're invisible to it.

2

Inbound Calls biggest bottleneck

When a lead calls and you're coaching or on a job site, they get voicemail. Maybe they leave a message. More likely, they call the next guy on Google. A pool job is $30K-$100K. One missed call is $30K gone.

3

Lead Qualification manual

When you do connect, you're spending time on calls that go nowhere. Tire-kickers, people with $20K budgets wanting $80K pools, HOA situations that won't work. Time you don't have to waste.

4

Site Surveys & Estimates manual

You drive out, walk the lot, take notes, come back, build a quote. Every estimate is hours of work before you know if they're serious.

5

Customer Communication opportunity

During a build, homeowners want updates. "When's excavation?" "Did the permit come through?" You're juggling texts and calls between managing the actual work.

6

Reviews & Referrals opportunity

Pool completion is the peak emotion moment. But you're already onto the next job. That window for a 5-star review and a referral closes fast.

The math is brutal: one missed call per week at a 20% close rate is two lost jobs per month. At $30K average margin, that's $60K/month walking away. You don't need to work harder. You need to capture what's already coming.
